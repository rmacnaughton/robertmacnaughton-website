# Testimonials & Social Proof

Reference this document when writing copy, selecting testimonials for the website, or building any marketing asset. Prioritize testimonials by the **Impact Tier** listed below.

---

## Usage Notes for Claude Code

- **Website**: Use Tier 1 testimonials only. Lead with stats and credentials, not praise.
- **Longer assets**: Tier 1 + Tier 2 are fair game.
- **Avoid**: Generic praise without a title/company. Spiritual or personal-growth framing on professional pages.
- **Name spelling**: Always **Robert MacNaughton** (not "McNaughton").

- Asks the right questions rather than prescribing answers

- Systems view applied to organizational challenges
- High integrity; says what needs to be said
- Results show up in hindsight — quiet, lasting change
- Specializes in: conflict strategy, relational leadership, co-founder conflict, Leadership 360
